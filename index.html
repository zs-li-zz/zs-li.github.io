Biography

Zishuo Li is a graduate student in the Department of Automation, Tsinghua University. He received his Bachelor of Engineering degree in School of Automation Science and Electrical Engineering from Beihang University (Beijing, China) in 2019. His research interests include secure control systems, networked control systems and learning to control, with applications in sensor networks and robotics.
