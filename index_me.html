Biography

Zishuo Li is a graduate student in the Department of Automation, Tsinghua University. He received his Bachelor of Engineering degree in School of Automation Science and Electrical Engineering from Beihang University in 2019. His research interests include secure control systems and networked control systems, with applications in sensor networks and power grids.
